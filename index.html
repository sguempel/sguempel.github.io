Professional Experiences

Nursing Background

Driven by a commitment to improving patient care, I began as a Medical Oncology nurse, witnessing both the dedication of healthcare professionals and the systemic barriers to optimal outcomes.

Seeking to make a broader impact, I transitioned to a Nurse Educator role in a drug adherence program for alcohol and opioid dependency. My oncology and pain management background enabled me to support patients through complex conversations, blending empathy with technical expertise. This experience ignited my passion for combining clinical insight with innovation.

Transition to Health Technology

As I took on more responsibilities, I began creating reports and analyzing data to improve our program, leading to my promotion to manager and expanding our team from 6 to 20 nurses. During this time, I was introduced to Salesforce and contributed to its implementation by identifying team pain points, defining requirements, and assisting in user testing—merging my clinical insight with my interest in technology.

This dual focus led me to join Cerner (now Oracle) as a business and test analyst on the Salesforce development team. I worked on developing a product that integrated client data within Salesforce Health Cloud. My responsibilities included creating requirements for RESTful APIs, performing hazard analyses to protect patient data, and ensuring HIPAA compliance. This role provided a solid foundation in interoperability and data integration, leading to my advancement to Product Manager, where I crafted product roadmaps to address evolving healthcare system needs.

Consulting Projects

Care Coordination & Referral Management

Implemented and customized Salesforce Service Cloud and Health Cloud solutions to enhance Care Coordination and Referral Management in contact centers. Developed workflows and automations to help nurses and providers seamlessly manage patient care from intake to scheduling, improving team communication, closing referral gaps, and boosting overall patient support efficiency.

Health Information Exchange (HIE) Enhancements

Guided several HIEs through Salesforce environment implementations, including a major migration from a for-profit platform to Nonprofit Cloud. Drawing on my experience across multiple HIE projects, I collaborated with the Salesforce team to design an implementation accelerator, which streamlined business processes and improved workflows across Sales, Project Management, and Service. This approach significantly reduced setup times and optimized operational efficiency.

Provider Management

Engaged in provider management projects that focused on improving workflows and data integrity, enabling healthcare organizations to maintain accurate, up-to-date provider records critical for quality care and operational efficiency.

Pharmaceutical Solutions

Contributed to the launch of an online pharmacy’s patient registration platform by working closely with developers to design a customer-facing portal. This portal enhanced user experience, streamlined patient registration, and adhered to compliance standards.
