Experience and Projects



Nursing Experience

As a Salesforce Consultant and Registered Nurse specializing in health and life sciences, I bring a unique fusion of clinical insight and technical expertise to every project. My background enables me to design and implement tailored Salesforce solutions that streamline business processes and elevate client outcomes. Leading all Salesforce projects at J2 Interactive, I drive innovation and efficiency, delivering measurable results for our clients.

Transition to Health Technology

As I became more involved, I started creating reports and analyzing data to drive improvements in our program, which eventually led to my promotion to manager. In this role, I grew our team from 6 to 20 nurses. It was during this time that I was introduced to Salesforce. I actively contributed to its implementation by identifying team pain points, defining requirements, and assisting in user testing—a unique opportunity to apply both my clinical insight and interest in technology.

This dual focus on clinical care and technology ultimately led me to join Cerner (now Oracle) as a business and test analyst on the Salesforce development team. I worked on developing a new product that helped clients integrate their data within Salesforce Health Cloud. My responsibilities included creating requirements for RESTful APIs, performing hazard analyses to protect sensitive patient data, and ensuring HIPAA compliance. This role gave me a solid grounding in interoperability and data integration, ultimately leading to my advancement to Product Manager, where I crafted product roadmaps to address evolving healthcare system needs.


Nursing Experience

Salesforce Consulting Projects


Care Coordination

Configured Salesforce Service Cloud and developed workflows to support Care Coordination contact centers, enabling nurses and providers to effectively manage patient care. By creating streamlined workflows and automation, I helped ensure that care teams could track patient needs, coordinate follow-ups, and provide timely support, enhancing the quality and consistency of patient care.


Health Information Exchange (HIE) Customizations

Guided several HIEs through Salesforce environment implementations, including one notable migration from a for-profit platform to Nonprofit Cloud. Leveraging my experience across various HIE projects, I collaborated with the Salesforce team to design an implementation accelerator, simplifying workflows across Sales, Project Management, and Service, and reducing setup times for new HIE clients.


Healthcare Referral Management

Supported multiple clients in optimizing their referral management processes, focusing on Health Cloud and Service Cloud integrations as well as Telephony solutions. By creating targeted automations and custom objects, I helped streamline communication across teams, ensuring that patient referrals were managed efficiently and appointments were scheduled without gaps or losses.


Health Confrence and Grant Management

Assisted clients that manage and host healthcare conferences by providing custom Salesforce configurations to support their product management, sales, and fulfillment processes. Developed custom objects and automations to streamline grant-related educational content, ensuring efficient tracking and delivery of resources aligned with conference goals.


Pharmaceutical Solutions

Contributed to the launch of an online pharmacy’s patient registration platform by collaborating with developers to design a customer-facing portal. This portal enhanced user experience, streamlined patient registration, and aligned with industry compliance standards.


Provider Relationship Management

Engaged in projects focused on improving provider management workflows and data integrity, enabling healthcare organizations to maintain accurate, up-to-date provider records essential for quality care and operational efficiency.
